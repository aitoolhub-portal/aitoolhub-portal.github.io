When to Seek Emergency Care

MediScan AI is designed for informational purposes only. Do NOT use this tool if you are experiencing a life-threatening medical emergency.

If you or someone else is experiencing any of the following symptoms, please call emergency services (e.g. 911 or your local emergency number) or go to the nearest emergency room immediately:

  • Severe chest pain or pressure, radiating to arm/jaw
  • Severe difficulty breathing or sudden shortness of breath
  • Loss of consciousness, fainting, or severe confusion
  • Signs of stroke (face drooping, arm weakness, speech difficulty)
  • Severe bleeding or coughing up blood
  • Pale, gray, or blue skin, lips, or nail beds

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about MediScan AI's privacy, security, and accuracy.

How does MediScan AI analyze symptoms? ▼

MediScan AI takes your reported symptoms, affected body areas, duration, and severity, then runs a weighted scoring algorithm to match your inputs against our curated database of common medical conditions. The results show a differential match percentage based on symptom alignment.

Is my health data private and secure? ▼

Yes. MediScan AI is built on a privacy-first sandbox architecture. All operations (symptom processing, diagnostic matching, and image preview analysis) happen entirely on your local browser. No medical data, patient info, or photos are ever sent to external servers, making it structure-compliant with standard data privacy regulations.

Can this tool diagnose disease? ▼

No. This tool is designed for educational purposes only. It is not a clinical diagnosis or medical advice. You should always consult a licensed healthcare professional for official diagnosis, treatment, and medical advice.

Can I share the generated reports? ▼

Yes. You can print the report or download it as a text file directly from the Results screen. This can serve as a detailed summary of symptoms to present during your consultation with a doctor.

    🔒 Privacy Policy & Data Security

    HIPAA & Sandbox Processing Information

    1. On-Device Local Sandbox: Your health data is processed entirely in the local memory sandbox of your device's web browser. The app runs completely client-side.

    2. No Server Transmission: We do not collect, transmit, store, or sell any information you enter into this app, including your name, weight, age, medical history, symptom selections, or uploaded photos. All image analysis and symptom cross-referencing are executed client-side.

    3. LocalStorage Persistence: If you complete an analysis, the report metadata is saved in your browser's local storage (`localStorage`) so you can review your history upon return. You can clear this history at any time by clicking "Clear All" on the landing page, which completely wipes the stored reports.

    4. HIPAA Alignment: Since no Protected Health Information (PHI) is ever transmitted to any server or shared with external parties, the platform matches standard health data privacy guidelines.
